Fix: Bible book selection dropdown not interactive

This commit addresses the issue where the Bible book selection dropdown was not interactive, preventing users from selecting books or scrolling through the list.

The problem was diagnosed as a complex interaction of z-index and stacking contexts, specifically exacerbated by the `backdrop-blur-sm` CSS property. The dropdown, despite having a high z-index, was not correctly capturing pointer events due to these stacking context issues.

The fix involved several steps:
1.  **Resolved race condition:** Moved book selection logic from `onClick` to `onMouseDown` to prevent the dropdown from closing before a selection could be registered. (This was the first fix attempt).
2.  **Removed `overflow-hidden`:** Removed `overflow-hidden` from the `<main>` element in `App.tsx` to ensure the dropdown was not being clipped. (Second fix attempt).
3.  **Explicit Z-index management:** Implemented a more robust z-index strategy in `App.tsx` to ensure the active view was always on top. (Third fix attempt).
4.  **Conditional Rendering:** Changed `App.tsx` to conditionally render only the active view, completely removing inactive components from the DOM to prevent any interference. (Fourth fix attempt).
5.  **Removed `backdrop-blur-sm`:** Removed the `backdrop-blur-sm` class from the header div in `BibleReader.tsx`. This was the ultimate fix, as this property was creating a new stacking context that caused pointer events to "fall through" the dropdown.

This series of changes, culminating in the removal of `backdrop-blur-sm`, has resolved the issue, making the Bible book selection dropdown fully interactive.
