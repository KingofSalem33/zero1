Project Vision Document for All-Natural Scented Lotions
Problem Statement
Local farmers market customers often seek products that enhance their well-being while supporting small businesses. However, they may struggle to find high-quality, all-natural scented lotions that provide a relaxing and rejuvenating experience. This gap presents an opportunity to create a product line that meets their needs while promoting local artisanship.

Target Audience
The primary target audience includes:
* Health-Conscious Consumers: Individuals who prioritize natural ingredients and are wary of synthetic products.
* Farmers Market Shoppers: Local community members who frequent farmers markets, looking for unique, artisanal products.
* Gift Buyers: Customers seeking thoughtful, natural gifts for friends and family that promote relaxation and self-care.
✅ Done → Now doing success metrics...
Success Metrics
1. Sales Volume: Achieve sales of at least 500 units within the first three months of launch.
2. Customer Feedback: Attain an average customer satisfaction rating of 4.5 out of 5 based on post-purchase surveys.
3. Market Penetration: Secure shelf space in at least 10 local farmers markets within the first six months.
