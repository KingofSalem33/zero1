## 1. CORE VALUE PROPOSITION (NON-NEGOTIABLE)

### Problem (1 sentence)

### Solution (1 sentence)
Biblelot reveals the "golden threads" connecting Scripture through AI-powered semantic search, multi-dimensional graph visualization, and narrative-level exegesis that shows WHY verses connect, not just that they do.

### Primary Outcome (1 sentence)
Users discover theological connections in seconds that would take scholars years to see, transforming fragmented verse study into deep understanding of Scripture's unified message.

### Value Formula

> **For** serious Bible students, pastors, and seminary students,
> **who struggle with** fragmented, surface-level verse study that misses deeper theological patterns,
> **Biblelot helps them** discover how Scripture interprets Scripture
> **by** revealing 11 types of connections through AI-powered graph visualization and KJV-only exegesis that treats the text as the ultimate authority.

## 11. ANTI-MESSAGING (WHAT YOU REFUSE TO BE)

This prevents dilution.

### We are NOT:

- ❌ **A devotional app** — We're for serious study, not daily inspirational quotes
- ❌ **Multi-translation platform** — KJV-only, honoring the text's authority and consistency
- ❌ **Commentary aggregator** — We let Scripture interpret Scripture; no external theology imposed
- ❌ **Social Bible app** — No sharing features, prayer walls, or community feeds; study is personal and deep
- ❌ **Reading plan tool** — We're for discovery, not completion tracking

### What This Means

- **We won't add** NIV, ESV, or other translations (maintain lexical consistency for Strong's)
- **We won't import** commentaries or study notes (Scripture-only authority)
- **We won't build** social features (avoid dilution into YouVersion territory)
- **We won't gamify** with streaks or badges (serious study, not habit-tracking)
